Intro

Mortal Kombat 11 is a 1v1 fighting game that you can play with your friends. The Game consists fights

Requirements

The things that you are going to need are a computer running Windows 7 or Windows 10, a Intel Core I5- 750 or newer or AMD Ryzen 3 1200 or newer, at least 8 GB of RAM, a NVIDIA Geforce gtx 670 (or equivalent) or newer, a broadband internet connection, and copy of Steam installed.

Installation

If you don't have Steam then go here and follow the instructions on to install Steam. When Steam is installed you will need to make an account so go here and make your account. When you have done that open your Steam app and sign in. When you are signed in you will go to the store in Steam and type Mortal Kombat 11 in the search bar at the top right of the store. When you have navigated to the page for Mortal Kombat 11 you will add the game to your cart. After that press purchase for myself, enter your credit card info and press continue. Then you will agree to the terms or service and press purchase. You now own the game. Next you will press the words that Install Content. Next you will tell the system where you want to install the game and press continue. After so waiting you press finish and wait for the game to complete installation. Then to start the game after you play, Play Mortal Kombat 11 then play again and the game will start.

Usage

Getting to the fight menu

When the Game has started you will see this title screen and press any button continue into the game.

After you have hit a butten to enter the game you will see this menu and press the picture that says fight.

This will open the FIGHT menu where you will either decide you want to play online fight against random people or a local fight a AI opponent.

Now you decide if you wanna fight the computer or a real person

  • Fight AI
  • Fight a Person

Fight AI

Since you have decided to fight an AI you will click on the picture that says local.

This will bring you to the character select screen press E to make it so that you are fighting AI. Next you will choose your character and style.

Now you will see this menu at the bottom or the screen, these are the maps that you can fight on. Feel free to choose any of them!

Fight a Person

First you will press the picture that says ONLINE

It will next you this menu that asks you to run a benchmark, press yes.

Next you will end up at this menu where you will press the VERSUS button under the KASUAL section

Next the game will start looking for some to fight and you will see this

Then you will press accept when the match is found on this screen

Last you will be brought to the character select screen where you will pick the person you want to fight as!

Basic Movements

Next lets cover the basic moves and a consept or 2

I on the keyboard is the HEAVY PUNCH

J on the keyboard is the LIGHT PUNCH

K on the keyboard is the MIDDLE/ LOW KICK (Depending on the character)

L on the keyboard is the HIGH KICK

SPACE on the keyboard is the GRAB

U+O on the keyboard is the X-RAY

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you have enough space
  • Make sure that you have a working Internet connection
  • Change the settings in the game so that your computer can better handle running the game
  • use the Steam verify files function on steam
  • Uninstall and reinstall if none of the other option works
